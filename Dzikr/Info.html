Dashboard Mechanics - Full Explanation

Dzikr

  • Four counters: Hamdalah, Tasbih, Takbir, Istigfar.
  • Each counter is incremented by pressing its respective +1 button.
  • Every 33 total of Hamdalah + Tasbih + Takbir:
    • Adds +1 Ijtihad
    • Adds +33 MP
    • The counters reset proportionally
  • Istigfar:
    • Every 100 points → adds +50 Ijtihad
    • Every 50 points → adds +5 WIS

Mujahadah An Nafs

  • Primary stats: INT (Intellect) and WIS (Wisdom)
  • Arousal levels: 1–6
    • Can be randomized with Awake
    • Can be manually defined with Define
  • Fujur Function:
    • Checks Arousal vs Istigfar requirements:
      • arousal=1 → Istigfar ≥50
      • arousal=2 → Istigfar ≥100
      • arousal=3 → Istigfar ≥500
    • Performs a CON save (roll 1d20):
      • Threshold 1 → 6
      • Threshold 2 → 13
      • Threshold 3 → 17
    • For "Arousal = 1" need to have "CON Save" more than first Threshold
      "CON Save" done automatic by Act the "Fujur".

Ijtihad & MP

  • Ijtihad = accumulated effort points from dzikr and Istigfar
  • MP = derived from Ijtihad:
    • Every +1 Ijtihad → +33 MP
  • Contributes to INT/WIS and can be used for other mechanics later

Progression Summary

  • Increment dzikr counters → earn Ijtihad & MP
  • Accumulate Istigfar → increases Ijtihad & WIS
  • Manage arousal with Fujur logic → discipline challenge
  • Monitor INT/WIS → primary stats for Mujahadah An Nafs
